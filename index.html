Our story

We built this because
we lived it.

Phones on the table during dinner. Children getting divided attention from parents who just had to reply to one work email. Evenings spent together — but not fully present.

We work with technology every day and believe deeply in its potential. But we also saw how easily it followed us home — into family time, conversations, and the spaces where life is supposed to unfold without digital interruption.

Aixode was created to protect those moments. Not by rejecting technology, but by changing our relationship with it.

At Aixode, we believe technology should serve life. Not the other way around.

FAQ

Common questions.

What is Aixode?
+
Aixode is a smart app that helps you and your family reduce screen time, break phone habits, and replace screen time with meaningful offline activities.
When will the app be available?
+
Aixode is currently in beta testing. Sign up for early access on our homepage to be among the first — for free.
Is Aixode available for iPhone and Android?
+
Yes — Aixode is being developed for both iOS (iPhone) and Android. Register your device preference when signing up.
Is there a free plan?
+
Yes! The free plan includes basic screen time tracking, daily overview, and one focus goal. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited blocking, coaching, and family features.
How does Aixode protect my privacy?
+
We only collect data you voluntarily provide, never sell your data to third parties, and comply fully with GDPR. You can request deletion at any time.
Can I use Aixode for my whole family?
+
Absolutely. Pro includes family sharing for up to 5 members, and Lifetime supports up to 10. Aixode was designed with families in mind from day one.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
+
Yes — no long-term commitments. Cancel your Pro subscription at any time and you'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
