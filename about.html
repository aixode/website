Our story

Built by people
who noticed.

Aixode was born from a simple observation — technology had followed us into the spaces where life is supposed to unfold without digital interruption.

Our story

We built this because
we lived it.

Phones on the table during dinner. Children getting divided attention from parents who just had to reply to one work email. Evenings spent together — but not fully present.

We work with technology every day and believe deeply in its potential. But we also saw how easily it followed us home — into family time, conversations, and the spaces where life is supposed to unfold without digital interruption.

Aixode was created to protect those moments. Not by rejecting technology, but by changing our relationship with it.

At Aixode, we believe technology should serve life. Not the other way around.
