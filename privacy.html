1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Aixode AS processes personal data when you visit our website or sign up for early access to the Aixode app.

2. Data Controller

Aixode AS · Org.nr.: 936 051 901 · Email: contact@aixode.com

3. Personal data we collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

4. Purpose of processing

Your personal data is used exclusively for:

  • Registration for early access
  • Sending relevant information about the launch and product updates

5. Legal basis

Processing is based on consent, in accordance with GDPR Article 6(1)(a). You may withdraw your consent at any time.

6. Data retention

Personal data is stored only as long as necessary to fulfil the stated purpose, or until you request deletion.

7. Data sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties, unless necessary for technical operations. We only use GDPR-compliant providers.

8. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your data. Contact contact@aixode.com.

9. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated when necessary. The current version will always be available on our website.
